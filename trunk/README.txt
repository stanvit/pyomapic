Based on Python OMAPI (DHCP) Interface by Perry Lorier <perry@coders.net> and
Matt Brown <matt@mattb.net.nz>: http://source.meta.net.nz/svn/dhcparpd/trunk/pyomapi/

On amd64 (x86_64) platforms libomapi, libds and libdst have to be compiled
with the -fPIC option.

Many thanks to Ignace Mouzannar (-ghantoos-) for his article on creating a deb from python distutils.
http://ghantoos.org/2008/10/19/creating-a-deb-package-from-a-python-setuppy/
